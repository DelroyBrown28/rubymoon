ABOUT RUBY MOON

Ruby Moon and Daughter Ltd. is a small independant family business founded in 1998. We pride ourselves on our customer service and friendly nature. Based in the top end of High Wycombe, near the train station, we are Wycombe’s best kept secret! The shops smells amazing! An aroma full of exotic incenses. It’s a festival, a happy go lucky, wonderful eclectic mix of colour, sounds and smells. It’s definitely not Eden, or any other clone shopping “experience”. Our stock is fair traded and ethically sourced whener possible. We have contact lenses, Alchemy Jewellery and lots of fairies. We sell Shisha Molasses and Charcoal, Hookahs and Pipes. We have a range of over 250 incences and more than 30 different rolling papers. Our clothing is fairtraded and colourful. We are one of the last outposts of alternative culture in High Wycombe. Be part of it and join us.
